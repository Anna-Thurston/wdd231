Music with Annie — Website Project Site Plan

Author: Anna Thurston — Date: August 8, 2025

Site Name

Music with Annie

This name represents my private music teaching business, which offers piano, violin, and early childhood music classes. It is personal, memorable, and clearly communicates that the site is about music lessons with a specific instructor — Annie. Using my name in the brand helps build trust and a personal connection with potential students.

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for current and prospective music students by offering clear information about private piano and violin lessons, early childhood music classes, scheduling, and pricing. It will also share the instructor’s qualifications, teaching philosophy, and testimonials from students. In addition, the site will feature a contact form for inquiries and a dynamically loaded FAQ or class schedule to help visitors quickly find the details they need.

Scenarios

  • How much do private piano or violin lessons cost, and what scheduling options are available?
  • When and where are early childhood music classes held?
  • How can I contact the instructor to ask about availability or register for lessons?

Color Schema

The site will use a clean, professional palette that reflects elegance and clarity, suitable for a music education business.

  • Primary Color – Black (#000000): Used for main headings, body text, and the navigation bar background.
  • Secondary Color – White (#FFFFFF): Used for page background and high-contrast areas behind text.
  • Accent Color – Light Gray (#CCCCCC): Used for section dividers, subtle backgrounds, and borders.
  • Highlight Color – Gold (#D4AF37): Used for call-to-action buttons, hover effects, and important headings.

Typography

The site will use two Google Fonts to create a professional, elegant, and readable design.

  • Playfair Display (serif): Used for all main headings (H1, H2, H3) to give a refined, classical feel suitable for a music education brand.
  • Open Sans (sans-serif): Used for body text, navigation links, and form labels to ensure clarity and easy reading on all devices.

These fonts complement each other by combining elegance in headings with simplicity and readability in the body content.
